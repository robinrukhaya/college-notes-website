ℹ️ About CollegeNotesHub

Built by students, for students

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to quality study materials. Our mission is to create a centralized hub where college students can find toppers' notes, previous year papers, and important question banks — all in one place, completely free.

Our Vision

To become the go-to study platform for college students across the country, helping millions of students score better in exams and reduce the stress of finding reliable study resources.

Why We Built This

As students ourselves, we know the struggle of searching for notes, papers, and question banks before exams — scattered across WhatsApp groups, Drive links, and random websites. This platform brings everything together in one organized place.

How You Can Contribute

Are you a topper? Do you have great notes or papers to share? Help fellow students by contributing your materials! Reach out to us through the Contact page, and we'll add your resources to the platform.
